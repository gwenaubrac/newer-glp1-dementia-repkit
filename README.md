# newer-glp1-dementia-repkit
Reproducibility kit for pharmacoepidemiology study to compare the risk of incident all-cause dementia in new users of GLP-1 agonists (semaglutide or tirzepatide) vs SGTL2 inhibitors, DPP4 inhibitors, and sulfonylureas in patients aged 60 or older with T2D and overweight or obesity.
